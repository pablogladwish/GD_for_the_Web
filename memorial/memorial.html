In memory of the scrollbar

The scrollbar was the best solution for displaying and interacting with content larger than the viewport. However with the greater and greater knowledge of how content on screen works the scrollbar was just to help you navigate the content, but then again the use of scroll functions on mouses and trackpads, which are generally more fluid and intuitive than scrollbars, means that the scrollbar has shrunk down into a grey bar on the side of your screen a residual smudge on your screen waiting to be wiped away in the next update.
